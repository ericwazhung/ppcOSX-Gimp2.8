6.22. Guides

Figure 16.81.  The “Guides” options of the “Image” submenu


The Guides submenu contains various commands for the creation and removal of guides.

6.22.1. Activating the Submenu

  • You can access this submenu from the image menubar through Image → Guides.

6.22.2. The Contents of the “Guides” Submenu

The Guides submenu contains the following commands:

  • Section 6.23, “New Guide”

  • Section 6.24, “New Guide (by Percent)”

  • Section 6.25, “New Guides from Selection”

  • Section 6.26, “Remove all guides”
